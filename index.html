Sweet and Spicy Fried Chicken Tenders with Sesame Dipping Sauce

INGREDIENTS

Chicken Tenders

  • 1 pound chicken tenders
  • 4 cups canola oil
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

Marinade

  • 3/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup red curry paste
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lemongrass paste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 3/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Dipping Sauce

  • 3/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 3/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoons fresh Thai basil
  • 1 tablespoon lemongrass paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha chili sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon peeled fresh ginger

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the Marinade by stirring together all the Marinade ingredients in a medium bowl.
  2. Add the chicken tenders to the bowl with marinade.
  3. Cover and chill for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  4. Use a large pan and heat up the canola oil to 330°F using medium-high heat.
  5. Remove chicken from marinade and discard any remaining marinade.
  6. Place flour in a zip-lock bag or bowl.
  7. Dredge chicken in flour, shaking off any excess.
  8. Gently place coated chicken in the hot oil.
  9. Fry chicken until coating is golden brown or a meat thermometer registers 165°F.
  10. Remove chicken from the oil and drain any excess oil using paper towels
  11. Cover chicken to keep warm until ready to serve.
  12. Prepare the Dipping sauce by stirring together all dipping sauce ingredients.
  13. Serve dipping sauce immediately with warm chicken tenders.
